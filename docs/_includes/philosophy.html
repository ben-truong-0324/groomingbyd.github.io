Hi! I’m Deanna

The face behind Vacaville Grooming Depot (also known as Grooming By Dee!)

With 12+ years of grooming experience, I truly love what I do and treat every pet like family. My goal is to create a calm, loving environment where dogs and cats feel safe, comfortable, and cared for during their visit.

At Vacaville Grooming Depot, we offer:

  • Private grooming rooms so we can focus on each pet’s individual needs.
  • A personal bather to help provide quicker turnaround times.
  • 100% hand drying for every dog.
  • Complimentary extras: Teeth brushing, ear cleaning, nail trims, anal gland expression, and brush out with every service.
  • Personalized care: We take pride in getting to know every pet parent and their pet’s unique needs.

I believe in moving at your pet’s pace, building trust, and creating positive grooming experiences that help pets feel relaxed and loved. We also offer experienced bathers who specialize in deshedding treatments to help minimize shedding at home!

My mission is simple: To provide a calm, safe environment that leaves pets happy, comfortable, and looking their best. If you’re looking for a groomer who genuinely cares, I’d love to meet you and your fur baby!

About Dee & The Shop

Hi, I’m Deanna (Dee), the owner and lead groomer here at Vacaville Grooming Depot! With over 15 years of professional grooming experience, I’ve built a shop designed around the needs of the pet.

A Quiet Environment

Unlike high-volume salons, our shop is quiet to minimize the anxiety that comes with multiple dogs and a chaotic environment.

One-on-One Care

I work with a personal bather so I can focus 100% on the grooming details. Every pet is hand-dried—never cage-dried.

Peace of Mind

I offer meet-and-greets for problematic pups and am willing to let you stay if you're worried about your pet's first visit.
